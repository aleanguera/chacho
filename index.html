The Secret of Duck Island: Special Edition

Part II

I see you have made it past the first hurdle - very good... very nice...

There is a second clue I want you to have now, this time - you will need to travel somewhere for it.

It may be close or far away, but it is something necessary for your next clue.

The next clue is the following:

Many dread the flight,

Others from my skull get a fright,

Not only am I useful despite,

But for some I am a delight.
